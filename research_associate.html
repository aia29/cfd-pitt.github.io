Dr. Arash Nouri

Dr. Arash G. Nouri is a Research Scientist at the Department of Mechanical Engineering and Materials Science in Swanson School of Engineering at the University of Pittsburgh (Pitt). He received his Ph.D. degree in computational modeling and simulation (CMS) from Pitt, his M.S. degree in chemical engineering also from Pitt, and his M.S. and B.S. degrees in mechanical engineering from Sharif University of Technology.
